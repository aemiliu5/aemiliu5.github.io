    Hello! I'm Aimilios Seimenis (aka Æmilius), a game programming student residing in Athens, Greece. I'm a technology enthusiast with a creative side, expressing it in the form of video games and photography.

    Being a gamer since an early age, a lifelong dream of mine has been to make games myself. In 2015, I released my first game called Nothing But Space on GameJolt and Google Play.

    In total, I've designed, programmed, and released 13 games in the span of 5 years, mostly for competitions like Ludum Dare, the Global Game Jam and the Extra Credits jam, where they've been recognized for their innovation, theme interpretation, and fun factor.

    I'm currently working on Dragamis, a serious game about type A diabetes.
